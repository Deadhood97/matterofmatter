The words said by a wise fellow hold true no matter the scenario you are a part of. Are you some rich snob who doesn't know what to do with their money? Indulge in some gold plated steak. And if you hear complaints, just tell the world to fuck off (Yes, just like Frank Ribery).<br />
Or are you struggling for the very existence of your body because you don't have a penny? In that case, you are bound to spend everything on the food you eat. So enjoy it to the max because it doesn't seem like you can have other luxuries.<br />
<br />
My point is food is universal. Every living entity requires food as a fuel to function. And hence if you want to live, you need to eat on a regular basis. Now if it was any other mundane thing like your job perhaps, you would hate it. But humans have found a way to transform this regular experience so as to not get bored while trying to survive. And that black magic my friend is what cooking is.<br />
<br />
The other day I was reading this article about what tastes good to hyenas. That article explained that hyenas, like all dogs, are scavengers and to them, the taste of rotten flesh is like a culinary explosion in their strong-jawed mouths. As you might have noticed from my introductory blog post that I am a fan of evolution. And I thank the events that shaped us into what we are today. One of the caveats of human evolution was for us to learn the art of cooking. What started by someone throwing edible stuff fire, has now been refined to the creation of delicious masterpieces.<br />
<br />
There is a lot of diversity on this planet which can't really be ignored if you have eyes and can see. Even if you don't, you ought to be aware of the fact that there is a lot of diversity on the planet. Examples include language, clothing, culture(wait, does that cover everything?), and yes, the way in which the ingredients are foraged, prepared and cooked. However, you would have also noticed that no matter the varied styles influenced by geographies, there exist some characteristics that bind them all. Think about shoes, everywhere in the world all the cultures evolved to have some kind of apparel covering their feet. Similar is the case of the art of cooking.<br />
<br />
No matter where you are right now in the world if you are told to imagine something being cooked, you would probably think of a heat source and some kind of vessel and of course the food in its less edible form. There is variation in the ways of preparation doughs, and their conversion into bread. But you can always find some version of bread. Meat, spices, vegetable, all follow the same formula. It can be related to the way we have scientific names for creatures on the planet( although, I do not know if we have scientific names for vampires because in that case, I should've used living things as a substitute term).<br />
<br />
Cooking seems complex since there are multitudes of things you have to consider. But if are adept enough, you know you just have to know your ingredients. You ought to know what doing something to an ingredient will result in. And while I'm writing this, I realize how ignorant this blog post is. I actually have very little clue about food. But I intend to learn more about it. Maybe then I'll write another blog post. Maybe then I'll come back to this blog post and laugh uncontrollably. So if you are reading this, hi future me! (so much cringe, my god) Hopefully, you've followed the path I intended for you. Or maybe a better one, or maybe a worse one (don't cry man, it'll be okay). Hopefully, you are okay. That's all I need.<br />
