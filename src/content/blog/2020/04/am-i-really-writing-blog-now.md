So you've found it. Are you really this bored that you have randomly stumbled to this awful thing that I call my blog? Bored or not, I'm happy that you've come to visit me. You'll soon find out that I'm not very original, most of what I do is pretty derivative. I really wish I was creative enough to provide you fresh insights on life and how I live it. But I'm just following the footsteps of people that have already trodden the same path and repeating the cycle of attempting certain stuff one does in order to spice up their life. You guessed it correctly, this blog is another one of those derivative phases which just so happens to be in "the bucket list" of countless around you. Fun fact, they call it "the bucket list" because after you complete the list, you are ready to kick the bucket. Oh man, if I was reader of my blog I would already call myself out on this pretentious bullshit.</div>
<div>
<br></div>
<div>
I guess that's enough self depreciation. And since you are still here after that very ironic first paragraph, lets see what I can offer with this blog of mine to the world. I like cooking. I love the satisfaction you get after you taste your own dish. I really love food. I'm open to new experiences food wise. Although the circumstances right now suggest I should stay away from consuming bats or pangolins for that matter.&nbsp;</div>
<div>
<br></div>
<div>
Next up, I love offbeat stuff. Anything weird, unique or peculiar, I would want to know. The world is full of surprises and even though all this means nothing in a grand scheme of things, it is still interesting to see how the world has shaped up to be. That's the reason I'm excited for the future despite me not believing that this stuff is of any value.</div>
<div>
<br></div>
<div>
What is of value is all the things that makes you happy. After all not many people have the capability of finding beauty in misery. I truly admire those that do. I feel like appreciation is the key to happiness. If you want to be happy, you need to learn how to appreciate small insignificant details around you. Appreciate the goods, appreciate the bads. Appreciate the fact that you can read what other's share on these blogs, on your screens, through your eyes, by reading these symbols we can all understand because some countries decided to invade the world hence uniting everyone. There are a million things that came together just so you could tap on a glass that makes a virtual chicken come out of a virtual hatchery to lay virtual eggs so that you can make virtual money which in turn allows you to create better virtual eggs and so on and so forth. It is truly amazing.</div>
<div>
<br></div>
<div>
Oh man, even while writing I couldn't keep track. So where was I? Ah yes, the things of value. Why am I not able to think of synonyms for the word 'thing', I don't know. The frequent use of the word 'thing' may/may not bother you, but it does bother me a little. If anyone is reading, please do provide some better options for me to use. So, the things of value. For me those are definitely my family and friends. My motivation to do anything is the people around me. That doesn't necessarily mean I need validation from them. I don't know how to explain, I am keeping this for future and I hope I can share my thoughts in a better way by then.&nbsp;</div>
<div>
<br></div>
<div>
I guess that was a good cue to complete this blog entry for now. I do have a lot of thoughts, this is just a glimpse of them, not even that actually. I really need to be sure of myself. Maybe that's another reason why I'm writing this blog. Well I hope someone accompanies me on this journey. Enough with the cliche, BYE!</div>
