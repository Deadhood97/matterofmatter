<p>&nbsp;To keep myself a bit active I started playing tennis an year ago. Its going well for the most part. It is a very interesting game, keeps me engaged. Anyway, this post is not exactly about tennis. But more about me waking up early and reaching the academy on daily basis. The academy around 1.5 km away from my home. When I started, I used to cycle there. And when I got a bike, that became the default mode. Occasionally I would take the electric scooter just for the heck of it. But for some reason my favorite way to reach there was always a walk. I guess I just like walking, absorbing all the things around me while also listening to Level1techs talk about the shit happening in the tech world, maybe enjoying a little music here and there along with it. And since the past week or two, I have completely shifted to walking. I wake up daily, drink a shot of filter coffee, visit the loo, collect all the necessary gear and I start walking. <br /><br />Now as I slowly experience the world around me on the daily walk, I must say it's not all rosy and mellow. The thing that gets on my nerve is all the shit on the road. Literal shit. Holy fuck man, there is so much shit in the world and especially where I live. Cow shit, dog shit, human shit and all the other kinds. It is impossible to avoid starting your day with walk without your eyes catching a good glimpse of shit on the road. And don't even try and walk straight without looking down, you are at risk of catching the gunk on your shoes/chappal/foot. I am almost sick of it. My love for walking is barely stopping me from transitioning back to bike.</p><p>This whole scenario reminds me of the saying, "Journey is more important than the destination". I am not sure if that is the exact saying but it definitely is somewhere along these lines. And I disagree somewhat. Journey is not always more important than the destination, it depends. Maybe it is, maybe it is not. But it definitely is a toss up. More often than not journeys are not as pleasant as they seem to be. Especially if the people accompanying you are not exactly to your liking. And especially if it is a return journey. i just want o reach home ASAP. I always wish for every researcher on earth to drop everything else and just focus on teleportation. I hope it is available soon and is affordable. Even if there is a risk of getting stuck between a wall while teleporting, I am more than willing to take it.</p><p></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;"><a href="https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEhgbYOY2se834rPHZcgjFxhWArsAN1kIT3DKYWoqnvcAUMNgnihtyyxZjcWGZYo7m9spMCmq3BX3kMBggGOizWvM3qizn667iC6TZABG_v4xzG0MKdaQxEKki5tEDD5SfwRgj2c_zTO1H8DP-Af_Pb8Sp7BASz9_nrPAM-3mGaC1PCZ1HvyVgIrsjRRng/s4032/fevicryl.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" data-original-height="3024" data-original-width="4032" height="240" src="/images/blogger/fevicryl.jpg" width="320" /></a></div>&nbsp;I just forgot where I was going with this. But this really makes you think that we accept the things around us very easily. Han bhyi koi kuch keh rha hai to shi hi keh rha hoga. We are so accustomed to believing most of what we are told, most of what we experience. Just looking around me I see an example. There are two paint bottles sitting on my desk. One is red, the other is blue. What is stopping me from calling the red blue and the blue red? Would I be wrong? These are just made up names anyway. Referring to these colours in a different language like laal and neela doesn't really make me wrong either. But everyone can guess what would happen if I refer to red as blue forever. People will question my ability to comprehend stuff. It's because I am rejecting status quo.&nbsp;<p></p><p>We are a weird bunch. And by we, I mean all the people on earth. We have grown so accustomed to the world around us that we crave even the slightest bit of originality. I was watching a video some time back about Yeezys, the Kanye shoes. And the guy was just going on and on about how these are so much different, so fresh, so original. because they do not conform to the well known, tried and tested shoe design language. Quoting all sorts of reasons like the ratio of hole sizes, lines of the shoe not converging and other shit. And he attributes the freshness to Kanye not being a typical shoe designer. He is new to the industry so he is very different, very fresh. It was interesting, I'll add the link if I come across the video again.But then again, were those shoes really any different? They still use soles, canvas, foam rubber and other conventional materials. So all in all they are pretty much 90% like the other shoes that exist already. My point is, the new wave is just the old wave, repackaged slightly differently. And then MBA folks just try very hard to make us believe that it is new. <br /></p><p>I don't know what to think anymore. I hate writing. It makes me feel horrible. Or maybe I only write when I am in a bad mood. For some reason I can't write when I am feeling happy. It just doesn't happen. I'll stop for now. It's not like I am doing anything original. Just whining about anything and everything. Fuck this journey, just make me a successful writer like Rowling so I can collect my regular paychecks too. And fuck it, not even giving this a second read. So if you spot any mistakes, its your fault.<br /><br /></p>
