For that you have to spend some on lenses and shit. Anyway, I'm certain you know what that big structure is. If you somehow don't live in a city, that is a big ass water tank high up in the sky. I want you to focus on the super blurry object that I've tried to highlight using that unnecessary red circle. And no, that is not a person who is up there protesting for some random agenda. It has been some time since I've seen someone on the top of the tank. People don't really protest this passionately these days. Nowadays roaming the whole city on tractors can be seen as a credible protest. And people expect changes (smh). Instead of a screaming person, that is a relatively inanimate plant. Yup, that is a full plant right there where the red circle is. I'm sure you have a lot of thoughts. It's such an absurd thing to witness after all. I have a lot of thoughts as well.</p><p style="text-align: justify;">&nbsp;Let's get the most obvious one out of the way first. How the hell did a plant grow in concrete at such an altitude? My only theory is that somehow after years and years enough soil got accumulated on the top of the surface for a random seed to sprout. Then the air brought the gifts of nourishment where the plant didn't have to fight for sunlight. And when the stars align, everything becomes possible.</p><p style="text-align: justify;">What troubles me is not knowing how that plant feels. Is it happy to be up there talking with higher winds? Does it feel the superiority of being taller than a tree that has been working its whole life and knows it could never get this tall?&nbsp; I don't know if the plant feels privileged or not. It might take it all for granted since it has been getting the survival basics for quite some time now. Or maybe it does know the odds it is overcoming to simply exist and is thankful for it. Maybe the plant does freak out about facing a storm everyday.</p><p style="text-align: justify;">It is different plant. Looks ordinary. The situation makes it shine. But who the fuck cares about this shit. There are probably thousands and thousands of people who go through the route daily. I can bet most of them don't even know there is such a thing. A lot of them would've seen it, but didn't really consider the absurdity of it. So what is it accomplishing? Does it exist solely for the purpose of being discovered by a super vehla person? Does it exist so that the person can write some stupid shit on it? I believe it exists for the sake of existing. Just because a ton of people these days are trying to find the meaning in their lives, doesn't mean it has to too. It is existing just to spite those who think about its existence. And that is perfectly fine.</p><p style="text-align: justify;">And even if the plant somehow feels that its existence is a burden, it's not like it will jump from that very tank. It can't possibly do that. If the people in the Municipal Corporation building, right below the tank btw, get this sudden idea of doing the work that they are supposed to do and send someone for the maintenance of the tank then they might assist the plant in lightening that burden. And maybe that is reason the plant is growing so tall. To catch their attention.<br /></p>
